Load and execute the hs-define-product skill for defining the product at the global level.

Read the skill definition at skills/hs-define-product/SKILL.md and follow its Process section step by step.

Steps:
1. Load context — read existing docs, codebase, product specs
2. Ask fundamental questions — WHO is this for, WHAT problem, WHY it exists, WHY now
3. Challenge assumptions — push back on scope, test boundaries, surface hidden assumptions
4. Write docs/product-spec.md — problem statement, target users, core capabilities, scope boundaries
5. Present for human review — do NOT proceed until approved
